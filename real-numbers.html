Chapter 1 – Real Numbers (Class 10 CBSE 2025–26)

1.1 Introduction

Real numbers are numbers that can be represented on the number line. These include both rational and irrational numbers.

1.2 Euclid’s Division Lemma (Repeated)

For any two positive integers a and b, there exist unique integers q and r such that: a = bq + r where 0 ≤ r < b

1.4 Revisiting Irrational Numbers (Deleted)

Proof that √2 is irrational: Assume √2 = p/q in lowest form → p² = 2q² → p even → p = 2k → q even → Contradiction
